Color Scheme

For the Colors, I chose to use Orange as the main Color to direct the attention of the user. I then use grey as a background color to help focus the attention on words. Words are all written in white unless they are meant to be a main focus point.

Topography

For the choice of Topography, I decide to use *font-family: 'Shadows Into Light', cursive* for my headings 1-3. This choice was made because it stands out as a wavey font. Since my main theme is fire, I figured something that incorperates waves would be stylistic. For headings 4-6 I chose to leave them as they are simply because once the font size is too small, it can be hard to read.
Also notice that for all heading sizes the color is #FF7F00

To incorperate fire as a central theme, when words appear on a page as you scroll down, they will appear as theough they have been burned into the page. The animation will be a flame growing from bottom left to the top right as they appear for each letter.

Buttons

For the Butttons, I chose to use the same button classes for bootstrap with just a slight variation. I gave a slight color change to the primary button to incorperate the main accent color #FF7F00. Then for the secondary button I incorperated my lighter grey color #808080. My goal in the future is to also have a fire animation on click.
